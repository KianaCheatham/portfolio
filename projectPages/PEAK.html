Diner in Blender

Designed and developed a mobile app for the Congressional App Challenge to help student athletes manage their time, track health habits, and maintain a sustainable balance between academics, training, and recovery. Inspired by our own experience as student athletes, the app focuses on preventing burnout and improving daily consistency.


Co-developed an iOS application using Swift and Xcode, taking the project from initial concept to a functional prototype. As a self-taught developer at the time, I was involved in all aspects of development, including UI design, app logic, and feature implementation.
