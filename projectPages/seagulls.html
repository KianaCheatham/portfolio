Flock

A personal project exploring interactivity and motion using Processing. I created the original illustration in Procreate, then brought it into Processing where I animated a flock of birds to follow the user’s mouse, creating a dynamic, trailing movement across the scene.


This project was inspired by my interest in observing seagulls at the beach and served as an experiment in combining digital illustration with interactive coding.
